PROFILE

ZEROBASEONE

5th generation K-POP boy group ZEROBASEONE, a 9-member project group formed by MNET‘s survival show “BOYS PLANET” The group consists of Sung Hanbin, Kim Jiwoong, Zhang Hao, Seok Matthew, Kim Taerae, Ricky, Kim Gyuvin, Park Gunwook, and Han Yujin.

The group's name means “a glorious beginning” for the nine members, starting with zero (0) and one (1).

Their debut album, “ZEROBASEONE The 1st Mini Album “YOUTH IN THE SHADE””, released in Korea in July last year, sold over 2 million copies, making it a stunning debut with a double million seller record.

Their next album, “ZEROBASEONE The 2nd Mini Album “MELTING POINT””, released in November, exceeded 2.13 million copies in first week sales alone.

They achieved the first ever feat as a K-POP artist, having two consecutive double million sellers since their debut and swept all the newcomer awards at all the awards ceremonies in Korea.

When they made their Japanese debut on March 20, 2024 with their first single in Japan, “Yura Yura - Unmei no Hana”, it broke the record for the highest sales of any debut single by a foreign artist, becoming an unprecedented hit.

On May 13th, the 3rd Korean mini album “You had me at HELLO” will be released, and the lead song "Feel the POP (Japanese ver.)” is also available! Also, this fall, they started their first overseas tour “2024 ZEROBASEONE THE FIRST TOUR” in Asia, including cities such as Singapore, Bangkok, Manila, Jakarta, Macau, Aichi and Kanagawa.
